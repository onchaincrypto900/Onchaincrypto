Are my funds safe if the app is deleted? ➕

Yes, your funds remain safe on the blockchain even if the app is deleted from your device. Your wallet app is just an interface to access them. You can always recover your wallet using your recovery phrase. Just reinstall the wallet and restore it using your phrase to regain access.
